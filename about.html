Why This Matters

Dogs drink water multiple times a day. Over their lifetime, that's thousands of gallons. If that water contains even trace amounts of harmful contaminants‚Äîlead, arsenic, bacteria‚Äîthe cumulative effect can be devastating. Kidney disease, digestive issues, weakened immunity, even cancer.

Yet most pet owners have no idea what's in their dog's water bowl. Municipal water reports are buried in technical jargon. Home testing kits are expensive. And when you Google "is tap water safe for dogs?" you get a thousand conflicting opinions.

DogBox changes that. We translate complex water quality data into simple answers. We connect you with affordable solutions. We give you peace of mind.
